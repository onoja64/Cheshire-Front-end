About

The Cheshire Cat is the cat of the Duchess. Alice meets it in chapter 6 from “Alice’s adventures in Wonderland”, when she leaves the Duchess house, and finds it in a tree. It constantly grins and can disappear and reappear whenever it likes. Sometimes it disappears and leaves its grin behind. In chapter 8 she meets it again in the Queen’s croquet garden. In “The Nursery Alice”. Alice notices that the cat has ‘lovely green eyes’. The Cheshire Cat is the only character in Wonderland who actually listens to Alice. With his remarks, he teaches Alice the ‘rules’ of Wonderland. He gives her insight in how things work down there.







Mission

The mission of the Cheshire Cat is to guide its community through the wonderland-esque landscape that is the cryptoverse. With increasing insanity with every step forward and choices that lead to dire consequences we need all the help we can get to all catch our personal white rabbit. The mystery of the Cheshire Cat lies hidden in meaning with an adventure ahead for the community. We will reveal more as our market cap expands, and the community strengthens as the foundation is built. As Alice once looked to the Cheshire Cat for guidance through wonderland, we must look to the cat for mysterious plans, fulfilled with buybacks, marketing and organic growth.
